# 🚀 SURE ODDS — MASTER PROMPT (SPORTPESA-STYLE)

> I am building a **sports prediction SaaS platform called SURE ODDS** with a user experience inspired by **SportPesa**, but focused on **odds prediction and analytics (NOT placing bets)**.
>
> The platform must feel:
>
> * fast
> * familiar to bettors
> * mobile-first
> * highly engaging
>
> It should mimic the **layout and interaction patterns of a betting site**, but instead of placing bets, users see:
>
> * predictions
> * probabilities
> * suggested bets
> * performance history
